Add a gamification system to the study planner with:

1. DAILY STREAK TRACKER:
- Count consecutive days of studying (completing at least 1 task)
- Show current streak prominently on dashboard
- Track longest streak ever
- Visual flame/fire icon that grows with streak length
- Streak freeze: 2 "skip days" per month to protect streak
- Daily reminder notifications to maintain streak
- Weekly/monthly streak statistics

2. ACHIEVEMENT BADGES:
Unlock badges for milestones:
- "Getting Started" - Complete first task
- "Week Warrior" - 7 day streak
- "Two Weeks Strong" - 14 day streak
- "Month Master" - 30 day streak
- "Century Club" - 100 day streak
- "Task Crusher" - Complete 50 total tasks
- "Centurion" - Complete 100 total tasks
- "Perfect Score" - Get 100% on any assessment
- "Honor Roll" - Maintain 90%+ average across all subjects
- "Early Bird" - Complete task before 8am
- "Night Owl" - Complete task after 10pm
- "Speed Demon" - Complete 10 tasks in one day
- "Subject Master" - Complete all tasks in one subject
- "All-Rounder" - Complete tasks in all 4 subjects in one day
- "Time Lord" - Study for 25+ hours total
- "Overachiever" - Finish all semester tasks

3. POINTS SYSTEM:
Earn points for activities:
- Complete task: 10 points
- Complete task with practice questions: 25 points
- Maintain daily streak: 5 points/day
- Study during Pomodoro timer: 15 points per session
- Get A on assessment: 100 points
- Get B on assessment: 75 points
- Improve grade from previous assessment: 50 bonus points

4. LEADERBOARD (OPTIONAL - can be private/friends only):
- Weekly leaderboard showing top students by points
- Filter by: Total points, Current streak, Tasks completed
- Friend comparison (add friends by code)
- School/class rankings
- Can make profile private if preferred

5. PROFILE STATS:
- Total study hours
- Tasks completed
- Current streak & longest streak
- Total points earned
- Badges earned (displayed like trophy case)
- Subject breakdown (hours per subject)
- Most productive day of week
- Average study session length
- Level system (Level 1-50 based on total points)

6. VISUAL REWARDS:
- Confetti animation when earning badge
- Level up animation with sound effect
- Streak milestone celebrations (7, 14, 30, 50, 100 days)
- Progress bars filling up toward next badge
- Badge showcase on profile
- Customizable avatar that levels up with you

7. MOTIVATION FEATURES:
- Inspirational quote of the day
- Progress comparison: "You're 15% more productive than last week!"
- Goals: Set weekly/monthly study goals
- Reminders: "You're 2 tasks away from 'Speed Demon' badge!"
- Share achievements (optional social sharing)

8. UI/UX:
- Dedicated "Achievements" page showing all badges
- Dashboard widget showing current streak and next badge
- Animated badge unlocking
- Clean, colorful design
- Mobile responsive
- Save all progress to localStorage

Make it fun, motivating, and addictive without being overwhelming!
